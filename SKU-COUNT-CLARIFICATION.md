## 🔍 WHAT THIS MEANS

### **1. Total SKUs: 55+**
**Definition**: Stock Keeping Units (includes ALL pack size variations)

**Example - Medjoul Jumbo Dates**:
- 400g Pack = 1 SKU
- 500g Pack = 1 SKU
- 800g Pack = 1 SKU
- 1000g Pack = 1 SKU
- **Total for this product** = 4 SKUs

**Definition**: Distinct product types (regardless of pack sizes)
